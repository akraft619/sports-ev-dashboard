How to read this

CLV (Closing Line Value) — did we get a better price than the market's final estimate before game time? Each pick is compared against the sharp closing line, not just whether it won.

Beat-the-close % — the primary edge metric. Random = 50%. At 50+ picks, beat-the-close above the threshold for that sample size (63% at n=50, 57% at n=200) indicates a real, systematic edge at p<0.05.

Why CLV beats ROI — win/loss at small samples is noise. CLV converges 3–5× faster and proves the process is sound regardless of short-term results.

Timestamps — all picks are posted to Discord before first pitch. Discord timestamps are immutable and publicly verifiable.
